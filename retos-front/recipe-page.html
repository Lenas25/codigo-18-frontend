Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This Classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or all
  • Optional filings:cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Beat the eggs, salt, and pepper together. Use 2 eggs for an average-sized omelette, 3 eggs for a large omelette, and 1 egg for a small omelette.
  2. Heat the pan: Melt the butter in an 8-inch non-stick skillet over medium heat. Tilt the pan to make sure the entire surface is well-coated with the butter.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour the eggs into the skillet. Let the eggs cook undisturbed until they just begin to set, about 1 minute. Run a heatproof spatula around the edge of the skillet to loosen the eggs.
  4. Add filling (optional): Add the filling of your choice. Cook for a few seconds longer, depending on the thickness of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Using the spatula, fold a third of the omelette over the center. Then, tilt the skillet and let the omelette roll onto a plate. Serve immediately.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
